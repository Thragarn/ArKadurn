These humanoids are brutish, broad-shouldered mountains of muscle with prominent jaws and distinctive tusks. Their culture is defined by constant conflict, where strength is the highest virtue and every member is prepared for the reality of combat. To sustain this lifestyle Ogresses have the most effortless pregnancies of all the races, often going to war while carrying twins or triplets, allowing a high birth rate that cancels out their high mortality.
Ogres come in various shapes and colors, but can be divided into three distinct subraces:
-[b]White Ogres[/b] are typically thick-skinned, thick-boned and thick-headed, with light whitish/grayish skin. Typically found in the mountain wildernesses and the undergrounds of Murd'Akhar, these ogres are much happier living with the drow than they were with the dwarves.
-The green [b]Swamp Ogres[/b] are considered the intellectual core of the ogres, dwelling in swamps and woodlands. Though the weakest among the orcs, they are still twice as strong as any human and can serve as master tacticians just as good as frontline warriors. Their elite shamans are experts in Totemic Magic, with  runes carved into totems that perform automatic casting, such as shooting fireballs or generating giant clay fists. They are present on all of the empire's fronts.
-[b]Orcs[/b] are the most physically imposing and ruthless of the kind, distinguished by their brownish skin and more prominent tusks. Living in massive nomadic savannah hordes, these natural warriors move in cycles. They travel west to raid Drascor, north to trade, and south to war with the Kahans. They are the elite fighting force of the empire, living in tents and remaining almost constantly on the front lines.
