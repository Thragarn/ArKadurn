The strangest of all humanoid races, Geletons are a monocellular race believed by scholars to be born from the closest survivors of the Urudshahag catastrophe. They typically appear as humanoid, boneless slimes, though their shape and color are as varied as their individual personalities. Instead of vital organs, Geletons possess only a coconut-sized core that functions as a head, containing their genetic information, brain, and eyes. All other processes occur within their plasma.
A Geleton's power level is unpredictable, as their initially weak bodies incessantly mutate to become more powerful and specialized. This adaptability is consciously guided and allows them to inhabit the most extreme environments in the world, from the mud wastes of Urudshahag, to which they are native, to the abysses of Lycoria and the tundras and volcanoes of Hirimgaard. Unsurprisingly, the Geletons' charming personalities helped them integrate into several societies, with a big community in Feijen and a smaller one in Drascor.
To communicate, Geletons vibrate a specific section of their membrane to produce speech in a unique voice.
