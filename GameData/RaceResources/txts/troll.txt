So tightly linked to stone that their connection borders on the religious, Trolls are a race whose every proverb mentions rocks and who, almost universally, have fallen in love with a gargoyle at least once. They are large humanoids that possess rocky shells growing from their backs and forearms, and their anatomy features long arms and short legs that grant them the unique ability to scratch their knees without bending or breaking their spines like centaurs.

Trolls possess immense strength and super-fast regeneration, yet they are bestowed with the most dull minds of all humanoid races. However, they stubbornly believe their intelligence is average, a delusion Gremlins are particularly fond of feeding. Native to the volcanic peaks of Eretyrn, the concept of nations is entirely foreign to Trolls who can be found wandering northern Drascor, Hirimgaard and northern Murd'Akhar, living solitary lives in isolation or acting as kind-hearted village guardians who protect locals from threats like wolves in exchange for food.

While they struggle to grasp basic mechanical concepts like the function of wheels, Trolls are surprisingly talented artists capable of creating works of unexpected beauty through music, sculptures, paintings or rocks stacked in impossible ways.
