Dopplers are an enigmatic and ancient lineage of pale, slender humanoids with thin, straight hair and almost featureless faces, feared for their ability to perfectly mimic the appearances of any humanoid they encounter. However, this natural state is rarely witnessed, as they spend the majority of their existence hidden behind the faces of others.

According to stories of the old world, this was the first artificial race ever created, hailing from the era of the father of sarcomancy, Mösin Dopp. The necromancer, hoping to grant the downtrodden of Urudshahag amongst whom he was raised a path to prosperity through the gift of shapeshifting, instead inadvertently condemned them and their descendants to this cursed form.
Yet despite the costs, Dopp's endeavor wasn no failure as Dopplers do now pervade everywhere humanoids dwell, hiding with such mastery that their true numbers and concentrations remain a complete mystery. And while some dismiss them as mere folklore meant to scare children, the more knowledgeable paranoid welcome their guests with silver cutlery and install silver door knobs, just in case...
