Small, very agile, and extremely smart, these ogroids are humanoid imps with elongated arms, large heads, and greenish skin. Despite their appearance and a deep passion for gold and explosives, Gremlins are empathetic beings whose numerous tricks are usually for giggles and rarely meant to hurt anyone outside the context of conflict. They are considered the smartest of all the hominids.

Gremlins are extremely capable of taking a joke and will often grin from one disproportionate ear to the other, showing a set of yellow, pointy teeth. Historically, many Gremlins mistrusted the Drow and migrated to Drascor and Hirimgaard, where they are highly valued for their engineering. Some even led goblin resistance movements against the startig Murd’Akhar empire. As freedom lovers and master pranksters, they are the finest engineers and explosive makers in all of Ar'Kadurn.
