Giants are the closest relatives to the Caeorn, appearing as oversized humans who have adapted to the brutal tundras of Hirimgaard. Their size grows proportionally to their power and results in a naturally high body temperature that makes them better adapted for survival in the sub-zero north. Aside from their massive scale and physical resilience, little distinguishes them from their human cousins in appearance.
Fully aware of the tactical disadvantage of their size against magic and archers, the majority of the race prefers to live in the isolation of their massive taiga villages as peaceful tribal hunters.

Despite this culture, the few Giants who venture out as adventurers or mercenaries are highly sought after for their sheer destructive power on the battlefield or against castle walls.
Their history is largely insular, with the death of the legendary Vahamon being one of the rare instances these massive humanoids stepped onto the world stage and played a main role in making history.
